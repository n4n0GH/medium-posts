The Crowdfunding is Over — Let’s Do This!

From January 4th to February 13th 2019 we ran a crowdfunding campaign for our 0xchan project. Like in many ICOs you were awarded with tokens in exchange for your Ethereum, but we didn’t require users to go through any KYC process because we didn’t want to restrict users in their freedom to support a project they believe in. KYC is also a perfect way to have your ID end up on the dark market.

The overall interest and support we got during that short timeframe was overwhelming to say the least; we are counting 182+ members on our Discord and a total donated amount of 241.6555 Ether. Is this a failure, considering our total goal of 2,000 Ether was not hit? No. Quite the opposite.

If anything, this is a clear indicator that people want this to happen and so do we.

While the crowdfunding was still going on we were gathering ideas not only from the development team but from our community as well. Direct interaction and discussion with our community is crucial to the platform’s success as we’re taking their input very serious — however silly that input might be at times.

Working together to create the tool and platform that user’s want to see and use is what we’re working on right now.

Our goal of 2,000 Ether was not met, but that doesn’t mean this project is dead in the water. Like we stated multiple times already, we’re making this happen, no matter what.

That said, we’re currently wrapping up features for 0xchan and finalizing wireframes so we can start building clickdummies to play around with. The road ahead is a wild one but we’re more than ready to take it on.

And last but not least a big fat thank you to everyone who believes in us and entrusted us with their Ethereum.

(you) are 0xchan!

I’ll be covering the development process of 0xchan through a series of Medium posts. If you want to keep updated or interact with our growing community, feel free to follow my Medium account, follow our Twitter or join our Discord.
